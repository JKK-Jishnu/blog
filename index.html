About Me

I like to call myself an Engineer but I wanted to be a scientist when I was a child. A Scientist who makes cool things. I was an avid reader when growing up. Life started to be interesting when I got admitted to college. It was a small college and I had to fight hard through pre-university merit system to get into it. I met and mingled with people from different sections of society and I started Identifying good resources. It may sound simple and trivial but let me assure you, once you are able to distinguish good resources, life will never be the same. If my memory is correct, I jio launched when I was into third year of my college.As Naval Ravikant put it, we live in a world with inifinite leverage, knowledge started exploding around me from then on. I worked as service engineer GCC for two years where I spent most of my time spent going around places and interacting with all kinds of people. I call this phase "exposure". Left the Job and came back to India, roamed around few places finally done with my travels, decided to settle down and do good engineering. Bengaluru it is, the mecca of indian engineering. great weather too atleast for people from this sub-continent.
